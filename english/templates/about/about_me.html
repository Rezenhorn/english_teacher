A certified teacher of English as second and foreign languages with 7 years of experience

More than 30 people achieved their goals in learning English with my help.

I strongly believe that with the right person and strong motivation you can master any skill. Well, if you are reading this, it means you have enough motivation to start, and I believe there is just the right person.

I am a certified teacher of English with a huge experience. My career path began when I was only 16 and helped my tutor with some of her students. I thought of tutoring as a job when I got a present for the Teacher's Day. I was a student myself but for someone I could have become a teacher! When I entered university teaching seemed a nice part-time job, so I started preparing students for the state exams in an online school. After a year there I felt experienced enough to work for myself and never regretted. Now I work with people of all ages and levels of language. I can find a perfect approach to any of them and even the shiest person will open up with me. I don't believe in methods that promise you a good level of English in two months, but I am sure that with a strong motivation and my help anyone will be able to reach their goals. In my job I use all techniques and sources. With my students we discuss controversial issues, listen to songs, watch TED talks and have fun. You might be afraid to start learning English because of your negative experience. Don't be! There is definitely a fun way to study!
